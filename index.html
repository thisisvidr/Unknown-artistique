About me

I am a person passionate by photography, making artworks and who loves to tell stories through visuals. I use my phone as a tool to capture the moments and emotions that move me. I believe that creativity is not limited by equipment, but by imagination. I enjoy experimenting with different genres and styles, from portrait to landscape. My goal is to share my vision and passion with the world through my photos and videos. I hope you like my work and follow me on my journey of expression and exploration.
